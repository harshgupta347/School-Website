Admission Process

Step 1: Online Application to be accessed through school website.

Step 2: Fill in the Application, attach the necessary scanned documents, pay the application fee and submit the form. (Incomplete forms will not be processed).

Step 3: Admission coordinators will verify the application and uploaded documents.

Step 4: Program Admission coordinators will Shortlist the candidates based on the eligibility criteria and Shortlisted Candidates would receive a mail or call from the admission coordinators and a provisional list of selected candidates will be uploaded on the website.

Step 5: Program annual Fee is assigned, and Fee payment link will be available in the admission portal. (The same is notified to the Candidates through an SMS from the School). Candidates are required to make the fee payment before the last date notified.

Step 6: All online fee payments will be duly acknowledged immediately and Students will receive an SMS confirming the provisional admission

Note : Fee Payments done in offline mode (NEFT, UPI, Bank payment) would receive an acknowledgment after 48 Hours. In case of delay you are required to contact the Admission coordinators .
